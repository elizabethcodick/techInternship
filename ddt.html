What is the DDT?

The Due Diligence Tool (DDT) is an application used by M&T Bank to perform due diligence on any potential acquistions of financial institutions.

How does it work?

The DDT takes credit data given by a financial institution to M&T and evaluates it. This helps M&T decide whether or not to make the purchase or obtain more data from the purchasing institution before deciding.

The Task

My objective was to create a Business Requirements Document (BRD) for this application. I walked through the application and analyzed it screen by screen to understand its functionality. I also used the JIRA reports and information I was provided from business user interviews to learn more about the application.
